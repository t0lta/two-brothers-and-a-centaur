# Hempen Rope (50 feet)
*Adventuring Gear*  

- **Cost**: 1 gp
- **Weight**: 10.0 lbs.

Rope, whether made of hemp or silk, has 2 hit points and can be burst with a DC 17 Strength check.

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 153. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.*
